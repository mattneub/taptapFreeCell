How TapTapFreeCell Works 3/4

Hint

To see what moves are possible, ask for a hint: tap with two fingers on the background. Everything is dimmed except Freecells and Columns with cards that can be moved onto a Foundation or a nonempty Column.

(If there is an empty Freecell or Column, any movable card can be moved onto it, so you don’t need a hint about that.)

If you ask for a hint and all cards are dimmed, and there are no empty Freecells or Columns, you are stuck. Use Undo to backtrack!

Card Finding

Long press on any card to highlight all cards of that rank. For example, if you want to know where all the Aces are, long press on any Ace.
