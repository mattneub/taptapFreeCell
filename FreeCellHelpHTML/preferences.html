Settings 1/5

TapTapFreeCell’s settings are designed to make your FreeCell play fast and efficient, while customizing the game action to suit your tastes.

To access TapTapFreeCell’s settings, tap the “ellipsis” button and choose Settings.

Sequence Moves

If Sequence Moves are on, TapTapFreeCell knows about using empty Freecells and Columns as intermediate stages when moving sequences from one Column to another. You can move an entire sequence of cards all at once.

If Sequence Moves are off, TapTapFreeCell enforces the strict letter of the law: you can move only one card at a time.

Supermoves

If Supermoves are on, TapTapFreeCell knows about supermoves. You can move an entire sequence of cards all at once from one Column to another, automatically taking advantage of empty Columns as staging areas for intermediate subsequences.

If Supermoves are off, TapTapFreeCell is ignorant of supermoves. To perform the equivalent of a supermove, you must manually play out the intermediate subsequence moves.
