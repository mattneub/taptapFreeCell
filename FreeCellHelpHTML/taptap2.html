How TapTapFreeCell Works 2/4

Automove-to-Foundations

Tap twice (double-tap) on the background to ask for Automove-to-Foundations. Tap-tap! All movable cards that can be moved safely to the Foundations will be moved.

A card can be moved “safely” to the Foundations if it isn’t needed to form a sequence. For example, the 4 of Hearts is not safe to move to the Foundations if the 3 of Clubs is not already in the Foundations — unless the 2 of Clubs is in the Foundations.

Of course, if a card can be moved to the Foundations, but not safely in this sense, you are free to move it anyway. Tap the card, then tap a Foundation. Tap-tap!

There is also a preference to perform Automove-to-Foundations automatically.

Undo and Redo

The Undo and Redo buttons are at the top right. Tap one to undo or redo the most recent move.

Long press on the Undo or Redo button (i.e. hold your finger down on it for about 1 second) to undo or redo all moves.
