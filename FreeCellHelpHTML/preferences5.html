Preferences 5/5

Automove On First Tap

If Automove On First Tap is on, then if you tap a Freecell or Column to start a move, and only one move is possible, TapTapFreeCell will complete the move for you immediately, without waiting for a second tap.

Show Clock

If Show Clock is on, the game timer is displayed at the top of the screen. To reduce your anxiety, the timer display is not updated continuously (even though the actual timer is always counting); it is updated only after you tap somewhere.

Animations

When cards move, the move can be animated. You have a choice of three animation speeds, or no animation (the cards jump).
