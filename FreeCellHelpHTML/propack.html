Power Pack

The Power Pack unlocks the full feature set of TapTapFreeCell.

Without the Power Pack, the game is perfectly playable, but many of the “intelligent” conveniences of TapTapFreeCell are missing.

Without the Power Pack:

  • Sequence Moves are off.

  • Supermoves are off.

  • Show Sequences is off.

  • Grow Tapped Card is on.

  • Tint Tapped Card is off.

  • Highlight Destinations is off.

  • Automatic Automove to Foundations is off. You can still double-tap on the background for manual Automove-to-Foundations.

  • Early Endgame is off.

  • Automove On First Tap is off.

  • Show Clock is off.

  • Hints are unavailable.

  • Card finding is unavailable.

If you attempt to use any of those features and you have not obtained the Power Pack, you are offered an opportunity to do so.
