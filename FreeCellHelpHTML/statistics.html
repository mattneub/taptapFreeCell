Statistics

The Statistics pane lists your played games. You can resume a lost game, view a game’s deal, and export a game.

To access the Statistics pane, tap the “ellipsis” button and choose Statistics.

Games List

Games are listed along with these features:

  • Date. The date and time when the game was played.

  • Time. How long the game took to complete. After a deal, the timer starts as soon as you make perform any action (such as making a move). The timer pauses when you leave the game, and resumes when you return to it and perform any action.

  • Moves. The number of moves you made. Sequence moves and supermoves performed by TapTapFreeCell count as a single move. Undo and redo do not count as moves. Automove-to-Foundation performed by TapTapFreeCell do not count as moves.

  • Won. Whether the game was won or lost. Time and Moves are not displayed for a lost game.

To sort the list, tap a column title at the top of the list. To reverse the sort order, tap the same column title again.

You can filter the list to display only numbered Microsoft deals. Tap the Numbered button. Numbered deals are displayed in numerical order. To unfilter, tap the Numbered button again.

Resume

You can resume a lost game. Tap the game to resume it.

(To replay a won game, export the game, mail it to yourself, and import it.)

Delete

You can delete a game from the list. Swipe left and tap Delete.

View

You can view a game’s deal as an image of the Tableau. Swipe left and tap View.

Export

You can export a game by emailing it. Swipe left and tap Export.
