# <%= title %>

The Power Pack unlocks the full feature set of TapTapFreeCell.

Without the Power Pack, the game is perfectly playable, but many of the "intelligent" conveniences of TapTapFreeCell are missing.

*Without* the Power Pack:

* [Sequence Moves](preferences#sequence-moves) are _off_.

* [Supermoves](preferences#supermoves) are _off_.

* [Show Sequences](preferences2#show-sequences) is _off_.

* [Grow Tapped Card](preferences3#grow-tapped-card) is _on_.

* [Tint Tapped Card](preferences3#tint-tapped-card) is _off_.

* [Highlight Destinations](preferences3#highlight-destinations) is _off_.

* Automatic [Automove to Foundations](preferences4#automove-to-foundations) is _off_. You can still double-tap on the background for manual [Automove-to-Foundations](taptap2#automove-to-foundations).

* [Early Endgame](preferences4#early-endgame) is _off_.

* [Automove On First Tap](preferences5#automove-on-first-tap) is _off_.

* [Show Clock](preferences5#show-clock) is _off_.

* [Hints](taptap3#hint) are unavailable.

* [Card finding](taptap3#card-finding) is unavailable.

If you attempt to use any of those features and you have not obtained the Power Pack, you are offered an opportunity to do so.
