### Automove-to-Foundations

Tap _twice_ (double-tap) on the background to ask for Automove-to-Foundations. Tap-tap! All movable cards that can be moved _safely_ to the Foundations will be moved.

A card can be moved "safely" to the Foundations if it isn't needed to form a sequence. For example, the 4 of Hearts is not safe to move to the Foundations if the 3 of Clubs is not already in the Foundations — unless the 2 of Clubs _is_ in the Foundations.

Of course, if a card can be moved to the Foundations, but _not_ safely in this sense, you are free to move it anyway. Tap the card, then tap a Foundation. Tap-tap!

There is also a [setting](preferences4#automove-to-foundations) to perform Automove-to-Foundations *automatically*.

### Undo and Redo

The Undo and Redo buttons are at the top right. Tap one to undo or redo the most recent move. 

_Long press_ on the Undo or Redo button (i.e. hold your finger down on it for about 1 second) to undo or redo _all_ moves.
