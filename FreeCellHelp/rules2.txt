### Movable Cards

Cards must be moved _one at a time_. The only cards you can move are:

* A card in a Freecell.

* The *last (bottom) card* in a Column.

<%= imageref("exampleLayoutAnnotated") %>

(A card played to the Foundations cannot be moved thereafter.)

Looking at the first Column in the layout pictured above, the 7 of Hearts may be moved but the 8 of Clubs may not — and so on, for all the Columns. Cards in Freecells are movable, so the 6 of Spades and the 7 of Clubs may be moved.
