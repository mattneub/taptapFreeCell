TapTapFreeCell is a [FreeCell](rules) implementation where you _tap_, never _drag_, to indicate what you want to do.

### Tap-Tap to Move

To make a move, you **tap twice**. Tap-tap!

1. First **tap** a Freecell or Column to select it. TapTapFreeCell is now _waiting_ for the second tap.

2. Then **tap** any legal place to **move** there.

    Or **tap** elsewhere (such as the background) to **cancel**.

I want to move the sequence in Column 1 (black 9-8-7-6-5) onto the red 10 in Column 2. First, I **tap** Column 1:

Because of my [settings](preferences), Column 1 grows a little and is tinted a little, to show it's selected; and everything is dimmed except for destinations I can legally move to. Column 2 is a legal place to move from Column 1, so it is undimmed. (Alternatively, I could move the sequence to the empty Column 7, or I could move one card to a Freecell, so they are undimmed too.)

To move the sequence from Column 1 to Column 2, I **tap** Column 2. Tap-tap!
