### Hint

To see what moves are possible, ask for a hint: tap with _two fingers_ on the background. Everything is dimmed except Freecells and Columns with cards that can be moved _onto a Foundation or a nonempty Column_.

(If there is an empty Freecell or Column, _any_ movable card can be moved onto it, so you don't need a hint about that.)

If you ask for a hint and _all_ cards are dimmed, and there are no empty Freecells or Columns, _you are stuck_. Use Undo to backtrack!

Hints are a [Power Pack](propack) feature.

### Card Finding

_Long press_ on any card to highlight _all_ cards of that rank. For example, if you want to know where all the Aces are, long press on _any_ Ace.

Card finding is a [Power Pack](propack) feature.
