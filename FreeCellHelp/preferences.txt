TapTapFreeCell's settings are designed to make your FreeCell play fast and efficient, while customizing the game action to suit your tastes.

To access TapTapFreeCell's settings, tap the "ellipsis" button and choose Settings.

### Sequence Moves

If Sequence Moves are _on_, TapTapFreeCell knows about using empty Freecells and Columns as [intermediate stages](rules5#why-the-freecells-matter) when moving sequences from one Column to another. You can move an entire sequence of cards _all at once_.

If Sequence Moves are _off_, TapTapFreeCell enforces the strict letter of the [law](rules2#movable-cards): you can move only one card at a time.

### Supermoves

If Supermoves are _on_, TapTapFreeCell knows about [supermoves](supermoves). You can move an entire sequence of cards _all at once_ from one Column to another, *automatically* taking advantage of empty Columns as staging areas for intermediate subsequences.

If Supermoves are _off_, TapTapFreeCell is ignorant of supermoves. To perform the equivalent of a supermove, you must *manually* play out the intermediate subsequence moves.
