### Automove On First Tap

If Automove On First Tap is _on_, then if you tap a Freecell or Column to start a [move](taptap), and only _one_ move is possible, TapTapFreeCell will complete the move for you immediately, without waiting for a second tap.

### Show Clock

If Show Clock is _on_, the game timer is displayed at the top of the screen. To reduce your anxiety, the timer display is not updated continuously (even though the actual timer is always counting); it is updated only after you tap somewhere.

### Animations

When cards move, the move can be animated. You have a choice of three animation speeds, or no animation (the cards jump).
