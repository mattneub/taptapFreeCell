### Moving Cards Onto Columns

The rules for moving cards **onto Columns in the Tableau** are:

* If a Column is empty, _any_ movable card may be moved onto it.

* Otherwise, look at the last card in the Column: only a movable card _one rank lower_ and of the _opposite color_ may be moved onto it.

<%= imageref("exampleLayoutAnnotated") %>

In the layout pictured above, the 6 of Spades (in the first Freecell) may be moved onto the 7 of Hearts (in Column 1), and the 7 of Clubs (in the second Freecell) may be moved onto the 8 of Hearts (in Column 7).

In this way, you form _sequences_ of alternating colors in descending order. In the layout, Column 1 ends with a sequence 9-8-7 of alternating colors.
